A Study on Abalone

Finding relationships between physical measurements and age in abalone data.

Analysis visualisation or model summary

Overview

The classic Abalone dataset connects shell measurements (length, diameter, and so on) with rings that proxy age. This study explores correlations and builds simple models to predict age from measurements, with attention to validation and interpretation.

What I did

Exploratory plots, correlation analysis, and regression or classification experiments as appropriate. Compared model performance and discussed which features mattered most.

Gallery

Exploratory plots, residuals, or model comparison

Tools & stack

Jupyter, pandas, scikit-learn, and statsmodels.
