Otari Wellbeing App

A Duolingo-style mental health app built with Flask and JavaScript.

App home or lesson flow

Overview

Otari encourages daily check-ins and bite-sized activities in a gamified loop: short sessions, streaks, and progress so building wellbeing habits feels approachable rather than clinical.

What I did

Flask routes for authentication, content delivery, and progress tracking; interactive front end in JavaScript; data model covering users, sessions, and streaks for the gamified learning loop.
